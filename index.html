About Me

Hi there! I am a PhD candidate at the Hyperspectral Remote Sensing Laboratory, Shenzhen University, advised by Prof. Sen Jia（贾森）. My research focuses on hyperspectral fusion imaging and intelligent interpretation driven by large foundation models. Passionate about bridging the gap between physical imaging and semantic understanding, I am dedicated to developing novel approaches that push the boundaries of hyperspectral remote sensing, spanning from low-level multi-modal fusion to high-level scene interpretation.

Open to collaboration. I am always open to academic collaborations in remote sensing, computer vision, and deep learning. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in working together. (Email: hewangquan2022@email.szu.edu.cn)

Research

I am interested in image fusion and computer vision, particularly in hyperspectral/multispectral fusion imaging and the adaptation of large foundation models for remote sensing intelligence.
